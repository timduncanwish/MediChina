The Process

How It Works

A clear, straightforward path from initial inquiry to treatment completion.

1

Contact & Basic Information

Fill out our online form and briefly describe your diagnosis and needs. We'll respond within 24 hours with next steps.

2

Medical Records Submission

Send us your existing medical reports (preferably in English). We organize and translate key information into Chinese for the hospital's review.

3

Hospital Review & Preliminary Opinion

We submit your case to the hematology specialist team. They review your case and indicate whether they can accept you and outline potential next steps.

4

Decide & Plan Your Visit

Based on the hospital's feedback, you decide whether to travel to China. We assist with appointment scheduling and basic visit planning.

5

Arrival, Treatment & Follow‑Up

We support you during hospital visits with interpretation and coordination. After discharge, we help translate key documents and arrange follow‑up consultations if offered.

FAQ

Common Questions

Are you a hospital? +
No. We are not a hospital or clinic. We are a coordination service that helps international patients connect with a leading hematology center in North China. All medical treatment is provided directly by the hospital.
Do you provide medical advice or decide on treatment plans? +
No. All medical decisions, diagnoses, and treatment plans are made only by licensed physicians at the partner hospital. We do not provide medical advice—we facilitate communication and logistics.
Can you guarantee a cure or a specific treatment? +
No. Whether you are eligible for a specific treatment depends entirely on the hospital's medical assessment of your condition. No outcome or treatment can be guaranteed. We advocate for you, but medical decisions rest with the hospital.
What costs should I expect? +
Our service fees cover coordination and support only. All hospital fees (consultations, tests, treatments, hospitalization, medications) are charged directly by the hospital. We can share approximate estimates based on hospital information, but actual costs depend on your condition and final treatment plan.
Can you help with visas, flights and accommodation? +
We provide general information for visa applications and local travel. The hospital may issue an invitation letter if applicable. However, you are responsible for applying for your visa, booking flights, and arranging accommodation.
Who should consider treatment in China? +
Patients with blood cancers or serious blood disorders who are seeking advanced treatment options, shorter waiting times, or a second opinion from experienced hematology specialists. Ideal for those open to international travel for healthcare.

Important Disclaimer

We are not a hospital, clinic, or medical institution and do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We are a coordination service only.

All medical decisions, including whether to accept a case, which treatments to use, and how to estimate risks and outcomes, are made solely by licensed physicians at the partner hospital(s).

Any information we provide about potential treatment options, durations, or costs is for reference only and may change based on your actual condition and the doctor's judgment.

We do not guarantee any specific medical result, cure, or improvement. Outcomes vary by individual case.
